Message from the Department Chairman

Dr. Rahat Hossain Faysal

Chairman, Department of Computer Science & Engineering

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science & Engineering! Our department is dedicated to fostering a dynamic learning environment where students can develop the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the ever-evolving world of technology. We are committed to providing a rigorous curriculum that balances foundational theory with hands-on, practical application.

Our faculty members are not only passionate educators but also active researchers and industry professionals who bring real-world experience into the classroom. This ensures that our students are well-prepared for the challenges and opportunities of the tech industry. We encourage a culture of innovation, collaboration, and critical thinking.

Whether you are a prospective student, a current one, or an alum, we invite you to explore the exciting work and research happening within our department. We are proud of the achievements of our students and faculty, and we look forward to the continued growth and success of our community.

About Us

In this modern world, to compete with global countries, we need to habituate ourselves with the use of technological equipment. So, opening such a department (CSE) was a notable decision. The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is therefore aware of providing quality education. The department provides an excellent and bright young brand of teachers who are deeply committed to the university to bring out the best graduates in academic disciplines and are contributing to the perfect development of the backbone of ICT of the nation. Our graduates are at the forefront in serving the nation with reputation. Currently, we are providing B.Sc. (Engg.) and MS degrees. However, we dream of commencing a PhD program as well.

Research Areas

From developing cyber security solutions to recommending potential medicine through AI, our researchers solve problems that impact the world.

Our expertise spans multiple disciplines, including Algorithms and Theory, AI and Machine Learning, Bioinformatics, Cyber Security, Database and Data Science, Natural Language Processing, Software Engineering, and Systems and Networking.
