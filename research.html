Research Areas

From developing cyber security solutions to recommending potential medicine through AI, our researchers solve problems that impact the world.

Our expertise spans multiple disciplines, including Algorithms and Theory, AI and Machine Learning, Bioinformatics, Cyber Security, Database and Data Science, Natural Language Processing, Software Engineering, and Systems and Networking.

Algorithms and Theory

Advancing the foundations of computer science with efficient algorithms and frameworks for solving computational problems.

Artificial Intelligence

Exploring cutting-edge AI for real-world applications in vision, natural language processing, and speech technologies.

Data Science

Leveraging computational models and algorithms to uncover insights from big data across various domains.

Cyber Security

Developing advanced security solutions to protect against evolving digital threats and cyber attacks.
